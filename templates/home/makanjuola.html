Aderemi Makanjuola Lecture Theatre

Aderemi Makanjuola Lecture Theatre, the building is designed to support a wide array of academic and non-academic functions, including receptions, conferences, and other special events.

The building was made possible by a gift from Aderemi Makanjuola in the year 2018.

Aderemi Makanjuola Lecture Theatre allows for a 500 person Theatre Style Setup with Car Parking Space for Guests.

Lagos State University Event Services coordinates events in Aderemi Makanjuola Lecture Theatre. If you would like to reserve a space, contact LASU Event Services for Reservations.
